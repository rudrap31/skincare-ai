Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2, 2025

SimplySkin ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password (via Supabase Auth).
  • Profile data: Skin type, concerns, and preferences.
  • Face scans: Images uploaded for skin analysis.
  • Scanned product data: Product barcodes and names.
  • Device and usage data: App version, device type, crash logs (anonymized).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide personalized skincare analysis and insights.

3. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your data. We only share data with:

  • OpenAI API: For AI-powered analysis (e.g., skin or product feedback).
  • Supabase: For secure user authentication and data storage.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.
